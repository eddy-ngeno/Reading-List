About This Project

My Reading List is a simple web application that helps you track your reading habits. Keep a record of books you've read, are currently reading, or want to read in the future.

Features

  • Add books to your personal reading list
  • Track your reading status for each book
  • Rate books you've completed
  • Filter and sort your collection
  • View statistics about your reading habits
  • All data is stored locally in your browser

Technology Used

  • HTML5 - For structure and content
  • CSS3 - For styling and responsive design
  • JavaScript - For application logic and interactivity
  • LocalStorage - For saving your book data in the browser

How to Use

  1. Navigate to the Home page
  2. Use the form at the top to add new books
  3. Fill in the title, author, and reading status
  4. If you've finished a book, you can also give it a rating
  5. Your books will appear in the list below
  6. Use the filter and sort options to organize your collection
  7. Click the edit icon to update a book's details
  8. Click the delete icon to remove a book from your list

Privacy Information

This application stores all data locally in your browser using LocalStorage. No data is sent to any server or shared with any third parties. If you clear your browser data, your reading list will be reset.

Future Improvements

  • Create reading goals and progress tracking
  • Add notes and reviews for each book
  • Implement data export/import functionality
  • Add cloud synchronization (would require backend)
